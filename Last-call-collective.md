# Last Call Collective

## Overview
Last Call Collective is the agency brand behind **lastcall.marketing** — a digital agency built for independent bar owners. Solo-operated by Ryan Stevens. Focus: ship fast, look great, make bar owners' lives easier.

## Niche
Independent bars, taverns, dive bars, cocktail lounges — owner-operators who need a real online presence without agency overhead or enterprise pricing.

## Services

### Custom Websites
- Built on Next.js 15 / TypeScript / Tailwind CSS v4, deployed on Vercel
- Mobile-first, fast-loading, SEO-ready out of the box
- Menu pages, event calendars, hours/location, online ordering or reservation links
- Hosting + maintenance available post-launch

### Web Design
- Full site design from scratch — layout, UX, responsive behavior
- Redesigns of existing sites (modernize dated bar/restaurant sites)
- Landing pages for promos, events, grand openings

### Design & Branding
- Logo design, brand identity, color/type systems
- Menus, flyers, posters, social templates
- Print-ready and digital deliverables

### Illustration
- Custom illustration work — branded characters, mascots, signage art, label/merch art
- Distinct visual style as a differentiator for bars competing on vibe

### AI Tools
- Custom AI tooling for bar operations — e.g. content generation for socials, menu description writing, automated review responses
- Lightweight internal tools/dashboards built to client need

## Positioning
Most independent bars are stuck with generic template sites (Wix/Squarespace) or no site at all. LCC offers custom, fast, design-forward sites at a price point that makes sense for a single-location bar — plus ongoing AI tooling that bigger agencies don't bother building for this market.
